Create your trip, invite your friends, add your objectives, and assign group gear.
Get Yr Beta makes planning easy and keeps your team safe.

Route Planning

Plan your route by entering your trailhead, objectives, and camp locations. Get Yr Beta makes it easy with built-in Google Maps and automatic coordinates.

Trip Roster

Add people to the trip by email address. Whether you're on a trip with a buddy or planning for the whole crew, Get Yr Beta will keep track of who's in.

Emergency Info

Maintain up to date emergency contact information for everyone on the trip. Gey Yr Beta will send the final trip plan to specified emergency contacts.

Packing List

Create a gear list and make assignments. Get Yr Beta will keep you organized and make sure everyone knows what they're supposed to bring.

About

The idea for Get Yr Beta stemmed from weekly meetings with the Boeing Alpine Club. Collecting numbers, route planning, and assigning gear took longer than anyone wanted. Get Yr Beta was born to streamline this process and get everyone out to 9lb Hammer sooner than later.
